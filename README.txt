Controls:
Download Page:
To download a song put a link to a youtube video into the textbox and click download.

Song List Page:
To select/play a song click the box to the right of the song length.
To delete songs click the same box but while holding either shift key.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

How to create desktop icon:
Right click "Catify.exe" and select "Create shortcut" then you can drag the shortcut to your desktop
and rename it to whatever you want.
